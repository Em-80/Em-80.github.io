Right to Repair

We believe that you should have the right to repair your own devices. The hardware you buy should be yours to modify, repair, and use as you see fit. We stand against the growing trend of locking down hardware and making it difficult or impossible for consumers and independent repair shops to fix things.

For this reason, we have chosen not to develop for iOS. We do not agree with Apple's restrictive practices regarding their hardware and software ecosystem. We encourage our users to support the Right to Repair movement and to learn more about this important issue.

To learn more, we recommend visiting the The Repair Association, a leading voice in the Right to Repair movement. Another great resource is the Rossmann Repair Group, which actively advocates for and practices the right to repair.
